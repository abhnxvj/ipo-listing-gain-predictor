How it works
  • Averaged gradient-boosting and random-forest model, trained on 309 Indian mainboard IPOs from 2022 to 2026.
  • Uses only the nine signals a retail investor can see by bidding day 3: issue size, early subscription, three-day GMP, and Nifty mood.
  • Scored walk-forward (train on the past, predict the future), so the accuracy above is leak-free rather than an inflated random split.
  • Educational only. Listing gains are noisy and the market regime shifts.
Educational project, not investment advice.
